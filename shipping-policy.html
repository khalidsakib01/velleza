Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Order Processing

Orders are typically reviewed and processed within 1 to 3 business days after successful order confirmation. During launches, campaigns, holidays, public closures, or unusual order volume, processing may take longer.

2. Delivery Areas

VELLEZA currently serves customers within Bangladesh unless otherwise stated. Delivery availability may vary depending on courier coverage, local access, weather, or security conditions.

3. Shipping Timeframes

Estimated delivery time is generally 2 to 5 business days after dispatch for most areas, but exact timing is not guaranteed. Remote or high-demand areas may require more time.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees, if applicable, may be shown at checkout, confirmed during order review, or communicated before dispatch. We reserve the right to adjust courier fees where necessary for special locations or oversized items.

5. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing an accurate name, phone number, district, city, and delivery address. Delivery delays or failed delivery caused by incorrect information may require additional confirmation or redelivery charges.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

If a customer cannot be reached, refuses the package without valid reason, or does not cooperate with delivery verification, the order may be delayed, cancelled, or returned.

7. Delays Outside Our Control

We are not liable for shipping delays caused by courier disruptions, severe weather, strikes, public holidays, government restrictions, political events, or force majeure situations.

8. Inspection on Delivery

Customers should inspect the package promptly after receipt. Any issue with packaging, damage, or missing items should be reported as soon as possible with supporting photos where available.

9. Contact

For shipping updates or delivery support, contact VELLEZA support with your order reference or transaction ID.
