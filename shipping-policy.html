Shipping Policy

Processing time

Orders are usually processed within 1 to 3 business days after payment confirmation. During launch periods, holidays, or promotional campaigns, processing may take a little longer.

Delivery window

Inside Bangladesh, standard delivery usually takes 2 to 5 business days depending on location and courier performance.

Shipping charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are shown at checkout or confirmed by our support team before dispatch.

Order tracking and contact

For shipping updates, contact our support team with your transaction or order reference. Keep your transaction ID after payment.

Delays

Unexpected courier delays, weather issues, public holidays, or address verification issues may affect delivery times.
