Refund & Cancellation Policy

Order cancellation

You may request cancellation before the order is dispatched. Once the order is shipped, cancellation may no longer be possible.

Refund eligibility

Refunds are reviewed for damaged items, wrong items, or confirmed payment issues. Requests must be reported within 3 days of delivery unless otherwise stated by support.

Non-refundable situations

We may refuse refunds for used items, damaged items caused after delivery, or requests made outside the allowed review window.

Refund timing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment source when possible. Processing time depends on the payment provider and bank.

How to request support

Contact support with your transaction ID, order reference, and a short explanation of the issue. Photos may be required for damaged or incorrect item claims.
