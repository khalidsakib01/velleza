Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Return Requests

Return requests must be made promptly after delivery. Customers should contact VELLEZA as soon as possible and provide the order reference, transaction ID if applicable, and a clear explanation of the issue.

2. Eligible Reasons

Returns or refunds may be considered where the wrong item was delivered, the item arrived damaged, the order was incomplete, or a confirmed payment issue occurred. Supporting photos or unboxing evidence may be requested.

3. Non-Eligible Reasons

Returns may be rejected for items that appear used, altered, damaged after delivery, missing original packaging where relevant, or reported too late for meaningful review.

4. Cancellation Before Dispatch

Orders may be cancelled before dispatch, but once an order is packed, assigned to a courier, or shipped, cancellation may no longer be possible.

5. Refund Method

Approved refunds are generally processed to the original payment source when technically possible. In other cases, an alternative method may be arranged at our discretion.

6. Refund Timing

Refund processing time depends on provider review, banking channels, and payment network timing. Approval of a refund does not guarantee immediate receipt.

7. Cash on Delivery Orders

For cash on delivery orders, any approved monetary resolution will be handled through a mutually acceptable process after return verification and internal review.

8. Abuse Prevention

VELLEZA reserves the right to refuse refund or return requests in cases of repeated abuse, suspicious claims, incomplete evidence, fraud risk, or non-cooperation during the review process.

9. Contact

To request support, contact VELLEZA with your order details, photos where relevant, and a brief description of the problem.
