You may contact us to request correction of inaccurate personal information connected to your order or account. We may ask for identity verification before making account-related changes.

8. Children

This website is not intended for use by children without supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where doing so would violate applicable law.

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the website after an update means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have a privacy-related question, you may contact VELLEZA support using the details shown on the website.
